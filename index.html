The Journey of Payam Yasaie

Full-Stack Developer

The Beginning

My journey into the world of development began in 2010. As an independent freelancer, I was driven by the desire to create. Over seven transformative years, I crafted solutions for various clients, building projects like the Dena Porcelain Store and the Star VPN Store using cutting-edge technologies of the time. This period was instrumental in shaping my foundational skills and understanding of the industry.

Stepping into the Energy Sector

2017 marked a pivotal turn in my career. I joined Tabesh Rayan Energy in Tabriz, Iran. Here, I was introduced to the complexities of the energy sector. Using PHP, Laravel 4, and MySQL, I developed a web application that would revolutionize online energy management. This platform wasn't just about code; it was about enabling engineers to make informed decisions, enhancing user experiences, and promoting energy-efficient building practices.

Leading the Way

By 2018, my journey led me to a leadership role at Tahlilgaran. Entrusted with a team of 5 talented developers, we embarked on ambitious projects, building two comprehensive e-commerce web applications using Laravel 6 and Vue. Beyond development, I found joy in mentoring, providing technical support through a Support E-Ticketing Web Application developed with Laravel 5, and innovating in the e-learning space with platforms integrated with headless Moodle LMS.

Mastering Python

2019 was a year of specialization. Joining Tas'hil Gostar, I delved deep into Python. Leading the development of diverse Python-based applications, I explored realms from payroll to account management. My work here involved designing and implementing database models using Odoo ERP, ensuring data integrity, optimizing performance, and ensuring scalability. It solidified my belief in the power of Python and its vast applications.

The Current Chapter

Today, I find myself thriving at OWOW Agency in Eindhoven, Netherlands. Each day brings new challenges and opportunities as I develop and maintain cutting-edge full-stack applications. My toolkit has expanded to include React and Next.js, alongside my trusted companions Laravel and Vue. Projects like the award-winning headless Shopify implementations showcase our team's innovation and my commitment to pushing boundaries. I've had the privilege of crafting custom Shopify applications, including an intricate pool cover configurator that delighted our clients. As I continue to explore technologies like Docker, AWS, and Vercel, I'm constantly amazed by the evolving landscape of web development. Looking back, I'm filled with gratitude for the journey so far and brimming with excitement for the chapters yet to be written in this ever-changing digital saga.

Skills & Passion

Over the years, I've cultivated a diverse skill set, mastering languages from PHP and JavaScript to Python and Java. Frameworks and tools like Laravel, Vue, React, and Odoo have become extensions of my problem-solving arsenal. But beyond the technical skills, it's the passion that truly defines my journey. The passion to tackle seemingly impossible challenges, to innovate in the face of complexity, to lead and mentor teams towards success. I thrive on optimizing performance, whether it's accelerating a Magento store by 70% or increasing software sales value by 400%. My commitment to continuous learning keeps me at the forefront of technology, always ready to embrace new tools and methodologies. It's this blend of skills and unbridled passion that fuels my journey, transforming lines of code into impactful digital solutions that make a real difference.
